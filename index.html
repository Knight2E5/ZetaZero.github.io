Collection of Problems in Undergraduate Math Competettions.

Abstract

This page collects typeset notes and problem sets spanning olympiad and qualifying-exam mathematics — algebra, analysis, and linear and abstract algebra chief among them. Material is organized the way a working problem set is organized: numbered, sequential, and without much ornament. Most documents are delivered as clean LaTeX-typeset PDFs, built from source problems that have been checked, translated, or verified before being written up.
